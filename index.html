1. Introduction

Welcome to SafariMate ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our SafariMate mobile application and related services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, profile picture
  • Identity Verification: Driver's license, vehicle information (for drivers)
  • Payment Information: Payment method details (processed securely by third-party providers)

2.2 Location Information

  • Current Location: Your real-time location for ride matching and navigation
  • Route Information: Pickup and destination addresses
  • Location History: Previous ride locations (stored securely)

2.3 Usage Information

  • App Usage: Features used, pages visited, time spent in app
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Log Data: IP address, browser type, access times, app crashes

2.4 Communication Data

  • Chat Messages: In-app messages between passengers and drivers
  • Support Communications: Emails, chat support conversations

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Core Services

  • Facilitate ride bookings and matching between passengers and drivers
  • Provide real-time location tracking and navigation
  • Process payments and manage financial transactions
  • Enable in-app communication between users

3.2 Service Improvement

  • Analyze app usage to improve user experience
  • Develop new features and services
  • Optimize route planning and matching algorithms
  • Conduct research and analytics

3.3 Safety and Security

  • Verify user identities and prevent fraud
  • Ensure platform safety and compliance
  • Investigate and resolve disputes
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 With Other Users

  • Passengers: Share driver information (name, photo, vehicle details, location) for ride coordination
  • Drivers: Share passenger information (name, photo, pickup/destination) for ride fulfillment
  • Chat Messages: Shared between matched users during active rides

4.2 With Service Providers

  • Payment Processors: Stripe, Razorpay for secure payment processing
  • Cloud Services: Firebase (Google) for data storage and analytics
  • Maps Services: Google Maps for navigation and location services
  • Communication: Push notification services

4.3 Legal Requirements

  • Comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • Respond to legal requests and court orders
  • Protect our rights, property, and safety
  • Investigate potential violations of our terms

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication and authorization
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Access controls and monitoring
  • Secure data centers and infrastructure

6. Data Retention

  • Account Information: Retained while your account is active and for 7 years after deactivation
  • Ride Data: Retained for 3 years for service and legal purposes
  • Location Data: Retained for 1 year for service improvement
  • Chat Messages: Retained for 90 days after ride completion
  • Payment Information: Retained as required by payment processors and tax laws

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 Access and Control

  • Access, update, or correct your personal information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out of marketing communications

7.2 Location Services

  • Control location permissions in your device settings
  • Choose when to share your location
  • Disable location tracking when not using the app

7.3 Communication Preferences

  • Manage push notification settings
  • Control email and SMS communications
  • Opt-out of promotional messages

8. Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with third-party services that have their own privacy policies:

  • Google Services: Maps, Firebase, Analytics
  • Payment Processors: Stripe, Razorpay
  • Social Media: Facebook, Google (for login)

We encourage you to review their privacy policies as well.

9. Children's Privacy

SafariMate is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy and applicable laws.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

  • Posting the updated policy in the app
  • Sending you an email notification
  • Displaying a prominent notice in the app

Your continued use of SafariMate after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
